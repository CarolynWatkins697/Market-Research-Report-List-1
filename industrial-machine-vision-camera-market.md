<p><strong>The Industrial Machine Vision Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Line Scan Camera</li><li>Area Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial machine vision camera market is primarily divided into line scan cameras and area scan cameras. Line scan cameras capture images one line at a time, making them ideal for continuous applications like conveyor systems, where they excel in high-speed and wide-area inspections. Area scan cameras, on the other hand, capture entire frames simultaneously, making them suitable for applications requiring detailed imaging of static scenes, such as quality control and object identification in manufacturing processes. Both types enhance automation and accuracy in industrial settings.</p></p>
