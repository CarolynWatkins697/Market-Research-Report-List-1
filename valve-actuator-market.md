<p><p>The valve actuator market consists of various types designed for specific applications. Hydraulic actuators utilize pressurized fluid, offering high force for heavy-duty operations. Electric actuators use electric motors for precise control and easy integration with automated systems, making them suitable for applications in tight spaces. Additionally, "Others" encompass alternative actuators like mechanical and thermal, catering to niche or specialized uses.</p></p>
