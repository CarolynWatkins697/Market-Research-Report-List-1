<p><ul><li>ASD Occluder</li><li>VSD Occluder</li><li>PDA Occluder</li><li>PFO Occluder</li><li>LAA Occluder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical occluder device market consists of various types designed to address specific cardiac abnormalities. ASD occluders target atrial septal defects, while VSD occluders are used for ventricular septal defects. PDA occluders close patent ductus arteriosus, and PFO occluders are designed for patent foramen ovale conditions. LAA occluders focus on left atrial appendage management, particularly in atrial fibrillation patients, preventing thrombus formation. Together, these devices enhance cardiac repair and improve patient outcomes in congenital and acquired heart conditions.</p></p>
