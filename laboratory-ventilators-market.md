<p><strong>The Laboratory Ventilators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CMV</li><li>AMV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laboratory ventilators market primarily includes Controlled Mechanical Ventilation (CMV) and Assist-Control Mechanical Ventilation (AMV) types. CMV delivers consistent breaths at preset intervals, ideal for patients requiring full support, ensuring precise tidal volumes. In contrast, AMV allows patients to initiate breaths, providing a blend of support while accommodating spontaneous breathing efforts. Both types cater to various respiratory needs in clinical and laboratory settings, balancing patient safety and ventilation efficiency for optimal outcomes in respiratory therapy.</p></p>
