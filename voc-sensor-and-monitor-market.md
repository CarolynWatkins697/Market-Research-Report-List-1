<p><strong>The VOC Sensor and Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The VOC sensor and monitor market includes various types, each serving specific applications. Electrochemical sensors detect volatile organic compounds (VOCs) through chemical reactions, offering high sensitivity. PID (Photoionization Detector) type sensors utilize ultraviolet light to ionize gases for detection, ensuring rapid response and accuracy. Metal oxide (MOS) sensors employ changes in resistance to identify VOCs, providing a cost-effective solution. Other types encompass a range of technologies, such as infrared and photoacoustic sensors, catering to diverse detection needs in various industries.</p></p>
