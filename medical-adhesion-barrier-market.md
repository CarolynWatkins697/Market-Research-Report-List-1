<p><p>The medical adhesion barrier market is segmented into three main types: film formulation, gel formulation, and liquid formulation. Film formulations provide a protective layer against tissue adhesion, often used in surgical procedures. Gel formulations offer a viscous texture that can conform to surfaces, facilitating easier application and better adhesion prevention. Liquid formulations are typically used for their fluidity, allowing for even distribution over surgical sites, ensuring comprehensive coverage and protection against unwanted tissue adhesion. Each formulation type addresses specific surgical needs and preferences.</p></p>
