<p><p>The digital inhalers market encompasses various types, primarily focusing on Metered Dose Inhalers (MDIs) and Dry Powder Inhalers (DPIs). MDIs deliver a specific dose of medication via a pressurized aerosol, providing precise control over inhalation. In contrast, DPIs utilize inhalation effort to disperse powdered medication, often requiring no propellant. Digital versions of these inhalers integrate technology to track medication usage, enhance adherence, and provide feedback to patients, improving overall management of respiratory conditions such as asthma and COPD.</p></p>
