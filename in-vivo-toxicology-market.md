<p><p>The in vivo toxicology market encompasses various testing methods to assess the safety of substances. Chronic toxicity testing evaluates long-term exposure effects, sub-chronic testing focuses on exposure durations of up to 90 days, while sub-acute toxicity testing examines impacts from exposures lasting less than 28 days. Acute toxicity testing, the most immediate assessment, looks at effects following short-term exposure, typically within 24 to 48 hours. Each type plays a crucial role in drug development and regulatory compliance, ensuring human and environmental safety.</p></p>
