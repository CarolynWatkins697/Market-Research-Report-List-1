<p><p>The 3D printing metal powders market primarily includes two key technologies: Direct Metal Laser Sintering (DMLS) and Selective Laser Melting (SLM). DMLS utilizes a laser to fuse metal powders into complex geometries, allowing for durable and lightweight structures, often used in aerospace and medical applications. SLM, on the other hand, fully melts the metal powders, achieving denser and stronger parts. Both methods cater to industries requiring precision and customization, driving innovation in metal additive manufacturing.</p></p>
