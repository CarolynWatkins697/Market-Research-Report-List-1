<p><p>The Cell Roller Bottles market comprises primarily two types: Polystyrene Cell Roller Bottles and PETG Cell Roller Bottles. Polystyrene bottles are lightweight, cost-effective, and suitable for various cell culture applications, offering good clarity and ease of handling. In contrast, PETG bottles provide superior chemical resistance, enhanced durability, and better protection against UV light, making them ideal for longer-term cell cultures. Each type serves distinct needs in laboratory settings, catering to the specific requirements of research and bioprocessing applications.</p></p>
