<p><p>The laparoscopic forceps market is categorized into two main types: single-use and reusable forceps. Single-use laparoscopic forceps are designed for one-time use, offering convenience, reducing the risk of infection, and eliminating the need for sterilization. The choice between these types often depends on factors such as cost, operational preferences, and considerations related to infection control in surgical settings.</p></p>
