<p><p>The Serum-Free Freezing Media market is divided into two primary types: DMSO-based and DMSO-free formulations. DMSO (Dimethyl Sulfoxide) is commonly used for its cryoprotectant properties, effectively preventing ice crystal formation during freezing, thus preserving cell viability. Conversely, DMSO-free media are designed to cater to concerns about toxicity and regulatory compliance, offering alternative cryoprotectants that ensure safe storage without DMSO. Both types are essential for applications in cell therapy, regenerative medicine, and biobanking.</p></p>
