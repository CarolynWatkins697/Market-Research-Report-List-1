<p><p>The thermal cycling thermoelectric modules (TEM) market comprises two main types: single-stage and multistage thermal cycling TEMs. Single-stage TEMs are designed for applications requiring a modest temperature difference, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, multistage TEMs utilize multiple thermoelectric elements to achieve greater temperature differentials, making them suitable for more demanding applications such as precision cooling and thermal management in electronics. Both types harness the Peltier effect for efficient heat transfer across varying temperature ranges.</p></p>
