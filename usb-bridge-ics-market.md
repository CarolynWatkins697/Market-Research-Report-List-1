<p><p>USB bridge ICs facilitate communication between USB interfaces and various protocols. The USB to UART bridges enable serial communication with microcontrollers, while USB to I2C bridges connect USB devices to I2C sensors. USB to SPI bridges allow fast data transfer with SPI-compatible devices, and USB to SATA bridges connect USB to storage solutions. USB to PCI/PCIe bridges extend USB connectivity to high-speed peripherals. Additionally, "Others" encompasses bridges for protocols like CAN, GPIO, and more, catering to diverse application needs.</p></p>
