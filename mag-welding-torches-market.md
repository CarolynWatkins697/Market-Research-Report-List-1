<p><p>The MAG welding torches market is primarily divided into two types: air-cooled and water-cooled. In contrast, water-cooled torches use water circulation to manage heat, allowing for prolonged usage without overheating. This makes them suitable for high-performance welding tasks where extended operation is required.
