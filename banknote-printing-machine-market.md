<p><p>The banknote-printing machine market comprises several types, including intaglio printing, offset printing, and others. Intaglio printing is the primary method used for high-security currency, utilizing engraved plates to create raised ink patterns for durability and anti-counterfeiting features. Offset printing, while less common for banknotes, is employed for producing bulk prints with vibrant colors. Other methods may include digital printing and screen printing, catering to varying security features and production requirements based on specific banknote designs and usage.</p></p>
