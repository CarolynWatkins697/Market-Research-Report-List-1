<p><p>Carton overwrapping machines are categorized into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual machines require operator intervention for wrapping, offering low output but high flexibility for small batches. Semi-automatic machines streamline the process, requiring minimal manual input while enhancing speed and efficiency, suitable for medium production needs. Automatic machines provide full automation, capable of high-speed operation with minimal supervision, making them ideal for large-scale packaging operations that demand consistency and high volume throughput.</p></p>
