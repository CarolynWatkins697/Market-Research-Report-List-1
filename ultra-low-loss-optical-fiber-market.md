<p><p>The Ultra Low-loss Optical Fiber market is primarily divided into two types: multimode fiber and single-mode fiber. Multimode fiber features a larger core, allowing multiple light paths, making it ideal for short-distance communication, such as within buildings. In contrast, single-mode fiber has a smaller core that enables a single light path, facilitating long-distance transmission with minimal loss and higher bandwidth. Both types are essential for efficient data transmission in telecommunications and networking infrastructures, catering to various application needs.</p></p>
