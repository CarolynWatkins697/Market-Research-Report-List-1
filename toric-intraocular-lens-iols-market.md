<p><p>The Toric Intraocular Lens (IOL) market is segmented into two main types: pre-loaded and not pre-loaded IOLs. Not pre-loaded IOLs require manual loading into the delivery device by the surgeon before implantation, offering flexibility but demanding skill. In contrast, pre-loaded IOLs come pre-packaged in a delivery system, allowing for quicker and easier implantation, reducing the risk of damage and ensuring precise positioning. Both types are essential for correcting astigmatism during cataract surgery, catering to diverse patient needs.</p></p>
