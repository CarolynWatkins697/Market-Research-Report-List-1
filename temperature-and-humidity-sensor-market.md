<p><p>The temperature and humidity sensor market is primarily categorized into capacitive and resistive types, along with other specialized sensors. Capacitive sensors measure humidity by detecting changes in electrical capacitance as moisture levels fluctuate. In contrast, resistive sensors gauge humidity based on variations in electrical resistance caused by moisture presence. Other market devices may include thermocouples and infrared sensors, offering unique advantages for specific applications. Each type plays a crucial role in environmental monitoring, HVAC systems, and industrial processes.</p></p>
