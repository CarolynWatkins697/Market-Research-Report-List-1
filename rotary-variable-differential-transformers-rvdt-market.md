<p><p>The Rotary Variable Differential Transformer (RVDT) market is categorized into AC and DC types based on the excitation method used. AC-RVDTs utilize alternating current to create variable inductance, leading to high accuracy and responsiveness, ideal for applications requiring precision in displacement measurement. Conversely, DC-RVDTs employ direct current, offering simpler integration with digital systems but may have limitations in sensitivity. These distinctions cater to different application needs in sectors like aerospace, automotive, and industrial automation, each selecting based on performance requirements.</p></p>
