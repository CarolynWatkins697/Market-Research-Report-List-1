<p><ul><li>Peripheral Dual Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry</li><li>Central Dual Energy X-Ray Absorptionmetry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-Ray Bone Densitometer market is primarily categorized into two types: Peripheral Dual Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (pDEXA) and Central Dual Energy X-Ray Absorptiometry (cDEXA). pDEXA is typically used for measuring bone density in peripheral sites such as the wrist or heel, offering a portable and cost-effective option. In contrast, cDEXA is utilized for central sites like the spine and hip, providing more comprehensive assessments. Both methods are essential for diagnosing osteoporosis and assessing fracture risk.</p></p>
