<p><strong>The Fume Hoods Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ductless Fume Hoods</li><li>Ducted Fume Hoods</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fume hoods market encompasses two primary types: ductless and ducted fume hoods. Ductless fume hoods are equipped with filters that purify air and recirculate it back into the laboratory, making them suitable for spaces lacking external ventilation. In contrast, ducted fume hoods are connected to a ventilation system that expels hazardous fumes outside, offering greater safety for handling volatile substances. Each type is designed to ensure a controlled environment, protecting users from harmful chemical exposures.</p></p>
