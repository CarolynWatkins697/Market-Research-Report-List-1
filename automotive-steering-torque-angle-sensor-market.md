<p><p>The automotive steering torque and angle sensor market comprises three main types: Steering Mount Sensors, Steering Base Mount Sensors, and Steering Column Mount Sensors. Steering Mount Sensors are typically integrated directly onto the steering mechanism, providing precise measurements of torque and angle. Steering Base Mount Sensors are positioned at the base of the steering assembly, offering reliable data for system control. In contrast, Steering Column Mount Sensors are located along the steering column, facilitating effective monitoring and enhancing the precision of electronic stability and steering systems.</p></p>
