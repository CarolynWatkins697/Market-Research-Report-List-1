<p><strong>The Clot Management Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inferior Vena Cava Filters(IVCF)</li><li>Neurovascular Embolectomy Devices</li><li>Catheter-Directed Thrombolysis(CDT) Devices</li><li>Percutaneous Thrombectomy Devices</li><li>Embolectomy Balloon Catheters/Clot Management Catheter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Clot Management Device Market encompasses several key types designed for efficiently addressing thromboembolic conditions. Inferior Vena Cava Filters (IVCF) are used to prevent blood clots from reaching the lungs. Neurovascular Embolectomy Devices target clots in brain vessels. Catheter-Directed Thrombolysis (CDT) Devices utilize catheters to deliver thrombolytic agents directly to a clot. Percutaneous Thrombectomy Devices physically remove clots through a catheter-based approach. Embolectomy Balloon Catheters/Clot Management Catheters aid in the removal or dissolution of clots, enhancing blood flow restoration.</p></p>
